Ferrari

The Ferrari 812 Superfast, replacement for the F12berlinetta, is one of the few exotic cars still equipped with a V12. The front-mounted 6.5L engine produces 800 horsepower and 530 lb. ft of torque and barely weighs 1600 kilos.

Bugatti

The Bugatti La Voiture Noire is a one-off supercar based on the Chiron. Unveiled at the 2019 Geneva Motor Show, it joins the Divo as a derivative from the already famous Chiron hypercar.
